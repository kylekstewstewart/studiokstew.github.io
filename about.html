Meet the Photographer

I’m Kyle D. Stewart, the eyes and mind behind Studio K-Stew. My journey into photography started on mountain trails and coastal roads—documenting wildlife, sweeping landscapes, and fleeting human moments. What began as a curious hobby became an obsession with light, texture, and raw, honest storytelling. Over the years, I’ve developed a versatile approach that blends adventure with precision, always chasing that one perfect shot.

Whether I’m waist-deep in a marsh at sunrise, capturing adrenaline at a sports event, or freezing a quiet portrait in soft window light, I aim to reflect the emotion and spirit of the scene. I believe a great photo doesn’t just document—it evokes. Studio K-Stew is more than a name—it’s a practice of patience, creativity, and grit. Thanks for stepping into my lens.
