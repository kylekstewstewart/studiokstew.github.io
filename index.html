Welcome to Studio K-Stew

I'm Kyle "K-Stew" Stewart — a New England-based photographer drawn to bold landscapes, remote places, and powerful motion. I’ve climbed 14,000-foot peaks before sunrise to capture first light over alpine ridges, and my action work documenting windsurfers during Hurricane Dorian has been featured in Windsurfing NOW magazine. My passion lies in travel and sports photography, but I’m also expanding into portrait work — always with an eye for authentic storytelling and wild beauty.
